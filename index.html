Jesse David

Jesse David is a current senior at Grand Valley State University and a Software Developer Intern at Terryberry in Grand Rapids Michigan. At Terryberry, Jesse works in an agile environment where he specializes in transferring data to Google Big Query, through custom software he helped develop, and then to Google Data Studio to be turned into professional reports and dashboards. Outside of work, Jesse is a member of the Grand Valley Swim & Dive Club and competed in his first ever Half Ironman on August 25th, 2019.
